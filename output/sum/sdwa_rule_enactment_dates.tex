\hline\hline
\textbf{Outcome} & \textbf{Original rule} & \textbf{Effective} & \textbf{Revision (cited previously)} \\
\hline
\addlinespace[2pt]
\multicolumn{4}{l}{\textit{Panel A: Rules in effect before 1985 sample start}} \\
\addlinespace[2pt]
Nitrate
  & 1975 NPDWR (40 FR 59570, Dec 24, 1975); MCL = 10 mg/L
  & Jun 24, 1977
  & Phase II Rule (56 FR 3526, Jan 30, 1991) reaffirmed same MCL \\[4pt]

Arsenic
  & 1975 NPDWR; MCL = 50 ppb (adopted from 1942 Public Health Service standard)
  & Jun 24, 1977
  & Arsenic Rule (66 FR 6976, Jan 22, 2001) lowered MCL to 10 ppb; compliance Jan 23, 2006 \\[4pt]

Inorganic chemicals
  & 1975 NPDWR; MCLs for barium, cadmium, chromium, lead, mercury, selenium, fluoride, and others
  & Jun 24, 1977
  & Phase II/V Rules (1991--1992) added contaminants; core framework unchanged \\[4pt]

Radionuclides
  & 1976 Interim Radionuclides Rule; covered gross alpha, Ra-226/228, beta/photon activity
  & Jun 24, 1977
  & Radionuclides Rule (65 FR 76708, Dec 7, 2000) revised MCLs; compliance Dec 8, 2003 \\[4pt]

Total coliform
  & 1975 NPDWR; coliform monitoring standard
  & Jun 24, 1977
  & Total Coliform Rule (54 FR 27544, Jun 29, 1989) restructured monitoring; compliance Jan 1, 1990 \\[4pt]

\addlinespace[4pt]
\multicolumn{4}{l}{\textit{Panel B: Rules enacted during 1985--2005 sample period}} \\
\addlinespace[2pt]
Surface/groundwater rule (SWTR)
  & Surface Water Treatment Rule (54 FR 27526, Jun 29, 1989); no pre-1989 equivalent
  & Jul 1, 1993
  & Enhanced SWTR (2002); LT1ESWTR (2005) \\[4pt]

VOCs
  & Phase I Rule (52 FR 25690, Jul 8, 1987); first MCLs for benzene, TCE, carbon tetrachloride, vinyl chloride, etc.
  & Jan 1, 1989
  & New regulation; not a revision \\[4pt]

SOCs
  & Phase II Rule (56 FR 3526, Jan 30, 1991) and Phase V Rule (57 FR 31776, Jul 17, 1992); first MCLs for atrazine, pesticides, etc.
  & Jan 1, 1993
  & New regulation; not a revision \\[4pt]

\hline\hline
\end{tabular}

\vspace{4pt}
\footnotesize
\textit{Notes:} Panel A outcomes (nitrate, arsenic, inorganic chemicals, radionuclides, total coliform) were regulated under the original National Primary Drinking Water Regulations (NPDWR), effective June 24, 1977, and are present throughout the 1985--2005 study period. The dates commonly cited for these contaminants (Phase II 1991, Arsenic Rule 2001, Radionuclides Rule 2000) refer to revisions that refined MCLs or expanded contaminant lists, not to initial regulation. Panel B outcomes (SWTR, VOCs, SOCs) represent genuinely new regulatory requirements introduced during the sample period. SMF = Standardized Monitoring Framework, established under the Phase II Rule (1991) with monitoring beginning January 1, 1993; it consolidated VOC, SOC, and inorganic chemical monitoring into a uniform national schedule.
